Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

The short version: Brain Boost Buddy has no accounts, no analytics and no tracking. Your child's name, PIN, settings and progress are stored only on your own laptop and are never sent to us — we couldn't read them if we wanted to. The only things processed online are your purchase (by Lemon Squeezy) and a one-time licence-key check.

1. Who we are

BASHBOP LTD (company number 14422092), 71–75 Shelton Street, London WC2H 9JQ, is the data controller for the limited personal data described below. Contact: info@bashbop.com.

2. What the app stores — locally only

  • Your child's first name (as you typed it at setup), your parent PIN, your gate settings, daily pass/lock status, and your licence key.
  • All of this lives in the app's local storage on your own computer. It is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and there is no account or cloud sync.
  • Deleting the app (or using Parent unlock → Switch off, then removing the app) removes the gate; local data can be fully cleared by deleting the app's data folder.

3. What is processed online

  • Your purchase — handled by Lemon Squeezy, our merchant of record. They collect your email, payment details and billing country to take payment, deliver your licence key and handle VAT. See the Lemon Squeezy privacy policy. We can see order details (your email and key) in our store dashboard; we never see card numbers.
  • Licence activation — when you enter a Lemon Squeezy licence key, the app sends that key and a short device label (e.g. "Brain Boost Buddy Mac") to Lemon Squeezy's licensing service once, to confirm it's genuine. Our own offline keys are verified entirely on your machine with no network call.
  • This website — hosted on GitHub Pages behind Cloudflare. We run no analytics and set no marketing cookies; those providers may keep standard technical server logs (IP address, request time) to operate and secure the service.
  • Email — if you email us, we'll have your address and message, used only to help you.

4. Children's data

The app is bought, installed and configured by an adult. The only child-related information is the first name a parent types at setup — and it never leaves your computer. We do not collect, see, or process any data about your child.

5. Legal bases

Where UK GDPR applies: performance of a contract (delivering your purchase and licence), and legitimate interests (operating and securing the website, responding to your emails, preventing licence fraud). We don't use your data for marketing and we never sell it.

6. How long we keep things

Order records are kept as long as required for tax and accounting law (normally six years). Support emails are kept only as long as useful for helping you. Everything else lives on your device, under your control.

7. Your rights

You have the usual UK GDPR rights — access, correction, erasure, restriction, portability and objection. Email info@bashbop.com and we'll sort it. If you're unhappy with how we handle your data you can complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk), but we'd appreciate the chance to fix it first.

8. Changes

If we ever change what the app or site collects, we'll update this page and flag material changes on the site. The promise we won't quietly break: your child's learning data stays on your laptop.
