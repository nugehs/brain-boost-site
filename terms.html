2. What Brain Boost Buddy is — and honestly isn't

Brain Boost Buddy is a desktop app for macOS and Windows that presents a learning challenge when your child's laptop starts, and unlocks the laptop when the challenge is passed or when a parent enters their PIN.

The honest bit: Brain Boost Buddy is a routine-building tool, not a security product. A determined or technically skilled child may find ways around it (for example by force-quitting or restarting the machine). It is designed to make learning the easiest path, not to be impossible to bypass. You should not rely on it for safeguarding, content filtering, or any safety-critical purpose — it does none of those things.
