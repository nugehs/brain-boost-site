FAQ

Questions parents ask

Will this stop a determined teenager?

No. A teenager with admin rights can force-quit the app. Brain Boost Buddy works best as a daily habit for primary-age children, not as something you can't get past.

For most kids, answering the questions is quicker than fighting the lock. For tighter limits, use your Mac or PC built-in screen time settings alongside it.

Is it safe to install on my child's laptop?

The macOS app is signed and notarized by Apple. Windows installs like any normal app. No tracking, no account.

How do I stop or remove it later?

Open the gate, tap Parent unlock, enter your PIN, and press Switch off Brain Boost Buddy. It stops locking at login. Delete the app if you like, or turn it back on later. Your licence stays valid.

Before you've finished parent setup, the app doesn't lock anything. You can quit it normally until your PIN is set.

What age is it for?

It's built around the UK Key Stage 2 curriculum for ages 9–11, with three difficulty levels: Year 5, Year 6 and Greater Depth. The Maths topics also cover common 11+ ground, and English follows the UK statutory spelling lists.

What happens if my child gets stuck and can't pass?

Wrong answers trigger a mini-lesson and a fresh question. If they run out of tries, use your parent PIN to grant another go, lower the difficulty, reduce the question count, skip today, or unlock the laptop.

Won't they just memorise the answers?

Questions are generated fresh each time. The numbers and wording change, so there's no answer sheet to learn.
